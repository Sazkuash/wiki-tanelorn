    HD Elunium (ID: 6241)

    Description

    A High Density Elunium that was buried deep underground.
    Used as material to strengthen armors from Refinement Levels 7 to 9.
    The density of this item guarantees that Refine attempts will not break the armor, and if the attempt fails, it will only regress by 1 level.
    Only a Blacksmith named Mighty Hammer can use this ore for Refining.
